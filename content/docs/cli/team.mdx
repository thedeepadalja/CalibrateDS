# Team Management Commands

These commands manage the `ptb-team.json` file.

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `ptb join` | Registers you in the team roster. If the roster is empty, you automatically become the Lead. Otherwise, you join as a Member. |
| `ptb team` | Shows the full team roster, roles, and current workload. |
| `ptb team role <handle> <role>` | Promotes or demotes a team member. (Leads only). |
